Welcome to New Hakimi Tin Works

History :

New Hakimi Tin Works (NHTW) has a rich legacy that dates back to its founding by Abdulhusain Bhai Daudbhai Sadikot in the town of Kutiyana. With a vision for quality craftsmanship and a commitment to excellence, he laid the groundwork for a thriving tin and aluminum products business. As the business grew, it was expanded by his son, Isaji Bhai, who moved operations to Rajkot. Under his leadership, NHTW flourished and became a recognized name in the region, known for its innovative designs and high-quality products. Today, the business is proudly continuing into its third generation, with Mustafa actively engaged in transforming NHTW for the modern era. Embracing digital advancements, Mustafa is committed to taking the business online, reaching new customers, and elevating the brand to new heights. NHTW remains dedicated to its core values of quality, service, and innovation, ensuring that the legacy established by Abdulhusain Bhai continues to thrive.
